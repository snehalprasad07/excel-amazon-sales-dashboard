# excel-amazon-sales-dashboard
This project presents an Amazon Sales Analysis Dashboard built in Excel to track and evaluate sales performance, order trends, customer demand, and operational efficiency across India. The dashboard integrates raw transactional data and transforms it into interactive visual insights such as monthly sales trends, top-performing products, city-wise orders, fulfilment performance, and size demand distribution. It enables stakeholders to monitor KPIs like revenue, order volume, delivery performance, cancellations, and returns to support data-driven decision-making.
# Project Objective
-	To analyse overall sales performance and revenue trends.
-	To identify top-performing products (SKUs), cities, and states contributing to revenue.
-	To evaluate customer demand patterns based on product sizes and categories.
-	To monitor operational efficiency through delivery rate, cancellations, and returns.
-	To compare fulfilment performance (Amazon vs Merchant).
-	To create an interactive Excel dashboard for quick business insights and reporting.

# Key Insights
-	Total revenue reached ₹79M, indicating strong overall performance for FY 2022.
-	Sales showed a peak in April–May, followed by a steady decline in later months. This suggests seasonal demand or reduced marketing/stock in later months.
-	Over 1.16 lakh units sold and 1.20 lakh orders, showing strong order volume.
-	Average Order Value (AOV) around ₹653, indicating moderate basket size. Opportunity exists to increase AOV through combos or upselling.
-	Delivery rate is 24%, which appears low compared to shipped orders.
-	15% cancellation rate is relatively high and may impact revenue.
-	2% return rate is low, indicating good product quality and satisfaction.
-	Top 10 SKUs contribute a significant portion of revenue. Indicates product concentration - few products driving most sales. These products should be prioritized for stock and promotion.
-	Sizes M, L, and XL show highest demand. Whereas very low demand for extreme sizes (3XL, 4XL, etc.). Inventory should focus more on high-demand sizes to avoid dead stock.
-	Major sales come from metro cities like Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Mumbai, and New Delhi.
-	Certain states contribute significantly more revenue. Opportunity to expand in low-performing regions.
-	Around 70% orders fulfilled by Amazon, 30% by Merchant. Amazon fulfilment dominates operations. Merchant fulfilment could improve delivery speed and customer satisfaction.
# Final Conclusion
This Amazon Sales Dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of sales performance, operational efficiency, and customer demand patterns.
-	Sales are seasonal, with peak performance in specific months.
-	A small group of products generates most revenue, highlighting key revenue drivers.
-	High cancellation rate suggests improvement needed in order confirmation or logistics.
-	Demand is concentrated in popular sizes and metro cities.
-	Amazon fulfilment dominates but Merchant fulfilment can be leveraged for better service.
# Recommendations
-	Focus marketing on top-performing SKUs and cities.
-	Reduce cancellations through better stock and delivery management.
-	Increase AOV via combo offers and cross-selling.
-	Optimize inventory based on size demand.
-	Expand into low-performing states for growth.
